Vineet Singh

Co-Founder & Chief Mentor

An investor, business owner and growth driver for some of the largest startups in India including 99 acres, naukri.com, MobiKwik, Buildzar among others, Vineet is an expert in developing scalable and profitable models across domains in new-world categories.

In his earlier avatar, Vineet was the CBO & COO at Mobikwik, a leading digital finance company in India. He led the evolution of the organization from a staff strength of 50 to 1500,increasing merchandise value from INR 100 billion to INR 1,500 billion and daily unique transactions from 50,000 to 600,000. The merchant base also increased in this period from 50,000 to 2.5 million.

During his entrepreneurial run at Buildzar, Vineet develop a successful online marketplace solution for home construction & renovation solutions. Buildzar raised INR 25 crores, having served over 650 homes. Prior to this, he has worked across 99acres.com, allcheckddeals.com, Naukri.com , NaukriGulf.com, NaukriFastForward & Jeevansaathi.com in leadership positions and leading the different verticals across geographies as vast as MENA & SEA giving him first-hand insights on the workings of these markets. At all his stints Vineet has curated many success stories across India.
