About

Narendra Kumar or ‘Nari’ is one of India’s leading designer in men’s wear since 2000. He is known for his perfect fit, contemporary themes and modern story telling. Nari’s structured silhouette and sharp tailoring keeps in mind his Indian roots. He has various brands that cater to different markets.
